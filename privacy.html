重點摘要
mreminder 是離線優先的鬧鐘 App，您建立的鬧鐘資料只儲存在您的裝置本機，我們沒有伺服器接收這些資料。為了確保 App 穩定性與營運，我們使用少量第三方服務收集當機紀錄、廣告 ID 與購買憑證（詳見下方）。

1. 我們收集哪些資料

1.1 您在 App 內建立的資料（不收集）

所有鬧鐘設定（時間、標籤、重複週期、震動、貪睡設定）僅儲存於您的裝置本機 SQLite 資料庫。我們沒有後端伺服器，不會接收或備份這些資料。

1.2 透過第三方服務收集的資料

資料類型用途處理服務是否可選
當機紀錄、堆疊追蹤、裝置型號 / OS 版本修正 App 錯誤、改善穩定性Firebase Crashlytics（Google）否（自動）
App 互動事件（畫面開啟、功能使用）了解使用模式、改善 UXFirebase Analytics（Google）否（自動）
廣告 ID（Advertising ID）顯示插頁式廣告Google AdMob是（購買 NT$120 移除廣告即停止）
購買憑證（receipt token）驗證購買、跨裝置還原RevenueCat + Google Play Billing否（僅在您主動購買時）

我們不收集：姓名、電子郵件、電話、位置、聯絡人、相簿、麥克風、相機、瀏覽紀錄、登入帳號、任何識別個人身分的資訊。

2. 資料安全

所有資料傳輸均透過 HTTPS / TLS 加密。本地資料庫無加密但保存於 App 私有沙箱（Android 系統強制隔離）。

3. 您的權利與資料刪除

3.1 App 內刪除（推薦）

進入 App → 設定 → 刪除帳號與資料，會清除所有鬧鐘設定、待觸發通知、主畫面小工具資料。

3.2 第三方服務的資料

  • Crashlytics / Analytics：解除安裝 App 並重置裝置廣告 ID 後，Google 會在 60 天內自動刪除關聯資料
  • AdMob：Android 設定 → Google → 廣告 → 刪除廣告 ID，即可斷開關聯
  • RevenueCat 購買紀錄：請寄信至 cjc305@gmail.com 申請刪除

English version

TL;DR
mreminder is an offline-first alarm app. Your alarms are stored only on your device; we have no servers receiving them. We use a small set of third-party services to collect crash logs, advertising ID and purchase receipts (see below).

1. Data we collect

1.1 In-app data (not collected)

All alarm configurations (time, label, repeat, vibration, snooze) are stored solely in your device local SQLite database. We have no backend server.

1.2 Data collected via third parties

Data typePurposeProcessorOpt-out
Crash logs, stack traces, device model / OSBug fixing and stabilityFirebase Crashlytics (Google)No
App interaction eventsUnderstand usage patternsFirebase Analytics (Google)No
Advertising IDShow interstitial adsGoogle AdMobYes - buy NT$120 IAP to remove ads
Purchase receipt tokenVerify purchase, restore across devicesRevenueCat + Google Play BillingOnly when you make a purchase

We do NOT collect: name, email, phone, location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, browsing history, login credentials, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Data security

All network traffic is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS. The local database is unencrypted but stored in Android app-private sandbox.

3. Your rights and data deletion

3.1 In-app deletion (recommended)

Go to App, then Settings, then "Delete account and data". This wipes all local alarms, scheduled notifications, and widget data.

3.2 Third-party data

  • Crashlytics / Analytics: uninstall + reset Advertising ID; Google deletes within 60 days.
  • AdMob: Android Settings, then Google, then Ads, then Delete advertising ID.
  • RevenueCat purchase records: email cjc305@gmail.com to request deletion.

4. Permissions

See section 4 above (zh-TW) for the full permissions table - same content.

5. Ads and purchases

The free tier shows a Google AdMob interstitial after every 5th alarm dismissal. AdMob uses your Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. A one-time NT$120 in-app purchase permanently removes all ads and stops sharing the Advertising ID with AdMob.

6. Children privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date above for material changes.

8. Contact

For any questions or data deletion requests: cjc305@gmail.com
